Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services

What services do you offer?
I offer comprehensive web design and development services including custom website development, responsive design, e-commerce solutions, SEO optimization, and ongoing maintenance. I can handle everything from simple brochure websites to complex web applications.
How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines vary depending on complexity. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects can take 2-6 months. After our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.
What's your pricing structure?
I offer both fixed-price projects for well-defined work and hourly rates for ongoing or flexible projects. After understanding your needs, I'll provide a transparent quote with no hidden costs. Small websites start at $1,500, with more complex projects priced accordingly.
Do you provide website maintenance?
Yes, I offer various maintenance packages to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. These can include regular updates, security monitoring, backups, content updates, and performance optimization.
What technologies do you work with?
I work with a wide range of technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (React, Vue, Angular), Node.js, PHP, WordPress, and various databases. I choose the best technology stack for each project based on its specific requirements.
How do we get started?
Getting started is easy! Simply contact me through the form on this website or via WhatsApp. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your project, goals, and requirements. From there, I'll provide a proposal and we can begin the exciting process of bringing your vision to life.
